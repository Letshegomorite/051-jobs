How to Write a CV in South Africa (2026)

A clean, ATS-friendly CV improves your shortlist rate. Use this layout and checklist before every application.

1) Use a clear structure

Keep sections in this order: Contact Details, Summary, Experience, Education, Skills, Certifications, References.

2) Keep contact details professional

Include full name, city/province, phone, and professional email. Skip unnecessary personal data.

3) Write a results summary

Use 3–5 lines with role focus, tools, and measurable outcomes (not generic adjectives).

4) Show achievements, not duties

Use: Action + Tool + Result. Example: "Reduced stock variance by 12% using Excel reconciliation."

ATS Optimization Checklist (2026)

  • Use job-title keywords naturally in summary and experience.
  • Stick to standard headings (Work Experience, Education, Skills).
  • Avoid tables, text boxes, and image-based CV layouts.
  • Keep date format consistent (e.g., Jan 2024 – Feb 2026).
  • Submit PDF unless the employer asks for Word.

Final Pre-Submission Check

Before applying, confirm your CV matches the role, includes measurable outcomes, and is readable on both mobile and desktop. Tailor for each application.
